Sergey Mikhaylovich Brin

The Early Years

Sergey was born in Moscow, Russia on August 21, 1973. Because of the religious discrimination his family faced, and the freedom that was hard to come by in Russia, his father decided to move the family to the United States. Sergey was 6 years old when he moved to the US.. He graduated from the University of Maryland in Mathematics and Computer Science and when on to grad school at Stanford University, where he met his Larry Page, his Google partner to be. At 43, Sergey has gone on to become the 12th richest person in the world.
